# SEO & Competitor Notes — Dave's Auto Center

## Reference: https://davesusa.com | https://lnk.bio/davesautocenter

### What They're Doing Well (steal for ProAutoTek)

**Link in Bio (lnk.bio/davesautocenter):**
- Uses lnk.bio as a central hub for all social/affiliate links
- Social presence: Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook
- Affiliate/partner links:
  - "Dave and Ali book" (Amazon)
  - Identifix/Direct-Hit (diagnostic subscription — Solera partner)
  - Mucar scan tool (mythinkcar.com affiliate)
  - Dragonfire Tool Storage (affiliate)
  - PPE Oil pans and Oil filters (their own Shopify store)
  - Banks Power promo (their own store)
- **Takeaway:** They monetize through a mix of affiliate links, their own e-commerce store, and influencer partnerships

**Website SEO (davesusa.com):**
- **Title:** "Dave's Auto Center" (simple, branded)
- **Meta description:** "Welcome to Dave's Auto Center. Utah's best mechanics."
- **Schema markup:** Full Rank Math schema — Organization + WebSite + Article + Person
  - Includes: name, URL, logo, publisher, author, dates, searchAction
- **OG image:** Storefront photo (1920x946) — professional, shows facility
- **Twitter card:** summary_large_image
- **Robots:** `index, follow, max-snippet:-1, max-video-preview:-1, max-image-preview:large` (max exposure)
- **WordPress + Rank Math** SEO plugin
- **Key messaging points:**
  - "Since 1990, family owned and operated"
  - "Best-equipped auto center and machine shop in the U.S."
  - "ASE certified technicians"
  - "State-of-the-art facilities"
  - "Transparent pricing, clear communication"
  - "Routine maintenance to complex repairs"

### Ideas for ProAutoTek

1. **Create a lnk.bio or similar link-in-bio page** — hub for YouTube, socials, product links, blog
2. **Add e-commerce** — sell diagnostic accessories, cables, subscriptions directly
3. **Affiliate partnerships** — TEXA direct, HP Tuners, tool storage brands
4. **YouTube channel** — diagnostic walkthrough videos (like Dave's does)
5. **TikTok presence** — short diagnostic clips, before/after fixes
6. **Robots meta:** Add `max-snippet:-1, max-video-preview:-1, max-image-preview:large` for maximum SERP real estate
7. **Enhance schema** — add Organization + searchAction like Dave's has
8. **Use storefront/workspace photo** as OG image (more professional than just logo)
9. **Add "Since [year]" and certifications** to copy for trust signals
10. **Partner/influencer model** — get automotive YouTubers to link to ProAutoTek products

### Schema Structure (Rank Math style)
- Organization (name, url, logo)
- WebSite (name, url, publisher, searchAction)
- WebPage (datePublished, dateModified, about, isPartOf)
- Article (headline, author, publisher, description)
