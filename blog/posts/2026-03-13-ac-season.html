Getting Ready for A/C Season: Why Shops Need the Right Service Equipment Now

By Mike Miller

Spring is here, and that means A/C season is right around the corner. Every year, repair shops see a surge of customers coming in with weak or broken air conditioning. The shops that prepare now — by ensuring their A/C service equipment is calibrated, reliable, and ready — will capture that demand instead of turning it away.

The Konfort Touch from TEXA is a professional-grade A/C service machine designed for modern refrigerants. It features a touchscreen interface, automatic leak detection, and precise refrigerant recovery and recharge capabilities. Whether you're working with R-134a or R-1234yf, the Konfort Touch handles it all with accuracy and speed.

Why does equipment matter this time of year? Because during peak A/C season, shops that can't diagnose and service climate control systems quickly lose customers to competitors. A slow, outdated machine means longer wait times, more comebacks, and frustrated clients. A modern, well-maintained service station means faster turnarounds and higher customer satisfaction.

TEXA's Konfort Touch also includes built-in vehicle databases and step-by-step guidance, reducing the chance of errors and helping newer technicians get up to speed quickly. It's not just about fixing A/C systems — it's about doing it efficiently enough to stay profitable during the busiest months of the year.

Don't wait until the first heat wave to check your equipment. Calibrate your machines, stock up on refrigerant, and make sure your team is trained on the latest procedures. The shops that prep now will be ready when the phones start ringing.
