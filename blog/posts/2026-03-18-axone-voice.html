The Axone Voice: Diagnostic Power Meets Hands-Free Efficiency

By Mike Miller

Imagine walking around a vehicle, hands covered in grease, and still being able to run a full diagnostic scan — without stopping to tap a screen. That's exactly what TEXA's Axone Voice delivers, and it's changing how technicians work.

Voice-Activated Diagnostics

The Axone Voice features AI-powered voice commands that let technicians control diagnostic functions hands-free. Need to check live data while you're under the hood? Just ask. Want to run an actuator test while you're at the back of the vehicle? Tell it what to do. The voice interface understands automotive context, so it's not like talking to a generic smart speaker.

Professional-Grade Coverage

Voice control is impressive, but it only matters if the diagnostic capability underneath is solid. The Axone Voice delivers comprehensive vehicle coverage across domestic, European, and Asian manufacturers. From engine and transmission to ABS, airbags, body modules, and ADAS systems — it covers the systems that matter on modern vehicles.

Efficiency That Pays for Itself

Time is money in a shop. Every trip back to a workstation to check data or start a test is time you're not billing. The Axone Voice reduces that wasted motion by bringing the diagnostic interface to wherever the technician is working. Over a week of typical shop use, that adds up to hours of saved time.

Built for the Shop Floor

The Axone Voice is designed for the realities of a working shop — it's durable, the voice recognition works in noisy environments, and the interface is intuitive enough for techs at any experience level. Newer technicians especially benefit from the guided diagnostic workflows that walk them through complex diagnoses step by step.
