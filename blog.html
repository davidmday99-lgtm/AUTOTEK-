March 11, 2026

Hybrid Vehicle Diagnostics: Bridging Two Worlds

By Mike Miller

Hybrid vehicles combine internal combustion engines with electric drive systems, creating unique diagnostic challenges that require expertise in both traditional and EV technologies.

Diagnosing a hybrid vehicle means understanding how the two powertrains work together. The hybrid control module manages the interaction between the engine and electric motor, deciding when to use each and when to blend them.

TEXA diagnostic tools provide comprehensive hybrid coverage, allowing technicians to diagnose both the ICE and electric sides of the powertrain. This includes battery analysis, motor testing, inverter diagnostics, and conventional engine diagnostics.

Why Software Updates Matter More Than Ever for Diagnostic Tools

By Mike Miller

A diagnostic tool is only as good as its software. Outdated software means missing vehicle coverage, unresolved bugs, and inability to access the latest diagnostic functions.

Some manufacturers release updates quarterly or annually. In a market where new vehicles arrive constantly, that can leave your tool behind.

TEXA takes a more aggressive approach with frequent updates that help shops stay current and ready for modern service work.

EV Charging Diagnostics and What It Means for Repair Shops

By Mike Miller

EV charging systems are becoming a major part of vehicle diagnostics. Repair shops that understand charging faults, communication errors, and battery-related issues will be better positioned for the future.

Modern diagnostic platforms help technicians identify charging problems faster and make more accurate repair decisions on new electric vehicle systems.

How Remote Diagnostics with eTRUCK Can Reduce Downtime

By Mike Miller

Remote diagnostics can change the way workshops and fleets manage service. With eTRUCK, shops can monitor connected vehicles, review status remotely, and address maintenance issues before they become major failures.

This reduces downtime, improves scheduling, and creates a better support experience for drivers, fleet managers, and customers.
