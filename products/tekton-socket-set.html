Tekton 230-Piece Impact Socket Set

1/4, 3/8, 1/2 Inch Drive Shallow & Deep 6-Point Impact Socket Set

Overview

Professional-grade 230-piece impact socket set covering 1/4, 3/8, and 1/2 inch drives with both shallow and deep lengths. Premium chrome-vanadium and chrome-molybdenum steel construction with radiused corners to prevent fastener round-off.

Key Features

  • 230 pieces — complete coverage for any job
  • Three drive sizes: 1/4 inch, 3/8 inch, and 1/2 inch
  • Shallow and deep lengths in every drive size
  • 6-point impact design for maximum grip on fasteners
  • Dual size markings — laser-etched and stamped for lasting identification
  • Radiused corners prevent fastener round-off
  • Fully stepped shoulders for strength and clearance
  • Drive tool retention for secure attachment
  • Lifetime warranty

Specifications

Premium steel construction: 50BV30 Cr-V • 4140 Cr-Mo • 6140 Cr-V • 8660 Ni-Cr-Mo • Black phosphate finish • Meets or exceeds ANSI/ASME standards
