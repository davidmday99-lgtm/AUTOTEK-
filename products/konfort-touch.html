TEXA Konfort Touch

Professional A/C Service Station for Modern Repair Shops

Overview

The Konfort Touch is a professional-grade A/C service machine with a touchscreen interface. Supports both R-134a and R-1234yf refrigerants with automatic leak detection, precise recovery and recharge, and built-in vehicle databases.

Key Features

  • Intuitive touchscreen interface for easy operation
  • Dual refrigerant support — R-134a and R-1234yf
  • Automatic leak detection to identify system issues
  • Precise refrigerant recovery and recharge every time
  • Built-in vehicle database with exact refrigerant specifications
  • Step-by-step guidance for every service procedure
  • Quick turnaround times to keep bays moving

Specifications

Touchscreen display • Fully automatic operation • EPA-compliant • R-134a / R-1234yf compatible
